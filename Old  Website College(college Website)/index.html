VISION

Sethupathy Government Arts College, Ramanathapuram will be a center for Higher Education to Enrich Enlighten and Empower to the youth from rural to build a strong and modern nation.

MISSION

Our Mission is to impart innovative and traditional Arts and Science Courses to the rural youth at an affordable cost and make every student employable through the development of right skills in the right time.

1. To provide quality education.

2. To provide skill based education.

ABOUT THE COLLEGE

Sethupathy Government Arts College, Ramanathapuram has been established by the Government of Tamilnadu in the year 1965 (GO MS. N o. 566 /EST Dept. Dated 14-7-1965) with undergraduate courses It is a Co-Educational Institution and situated in a rural area. The College was affiliated to the Madurai Kamarajar University, from 1965 to 2003 and has been subsequently Re-affiliated (may 2003) to the newly established University of Alagappa University
